Kamphoefner Mural
Digital, Canva.
Nov 17, 2024 (ADN 111)

The Kamphoefner Building is an academic building on NC State University campus. One of its facades has an interesting shape, featuring two square-shaped windows near the top.
This mural design was specifically designed for the Kamphoefner wall. It takes inspiration from Barbara Kruger's work, which is characterized by bold text, red highlight, and a black-and-white background. The text and background of the final design reflect the spirit of NCSU campus life and academics.

This project was an exercise in prompt engineering. The following parameters were desirable:

  • Retro-futuristic library, blending 1950s aesthetic elements with futuristic technology

  • A person is present to illustrate how the space is used
